About Us

Our Vision:

Through Crypto Done Right, we as a part of the security community, are trying to help professionals or enthusiasts in the space of Software Development, I.T or Management get comfortable with the complex world of Cryptography through a community driven knowledge base that is to-the-point, trustworthy and transparent.

Our Values:

1. Security:
Security is only as strong as your weakest link. Security is complicated and of paramount importance for any organization or product and there are very little room for errors. Security needs to be a part of everyone's responsibility. Now more than ever, earning customer's trust in any business translates to un-complicated and foolproof security.

2. Cryptography has its weaknesses too:
Like anything else in this world, cryptography is not perfect. There are multiple reasons why this is the case. But there are still various flavours of cryptographic algorithms that are proven to be secure against a wide range of attacks. Through this knowledge base, we aim to ensure that any professional who wants to implement cryptography is up-to-date and is aware of which flavors to avoid, which configuration to setup, how to move to stronger cryptography etc. All of this is delivered as easy to understand and structured content.

3. Change is the only constant:
Security is adaptive and is based on context. Different organizational values lead to different security measures and cryptography is an ever-changing landscape. Security (including cryptography) can never be achieved with an one-time setup. Like a well oiled machines, it needs to be maintained and tweaked from time to time.

Methods:

1. Demystifying Cryptography:
Cryptography is a complicated subject. What makes it complicated to consumers are the plethora of options (in terms of configuration and deploying) that exist to achieve most up-to-date and proven security. Everday use of cryptography for the majority of professionals does not necessitate understanding the intricate details of the various flavors available. Through Crypto Done Right, we aim to deliver content to our audience in the a clear and coherent fashion.

2. Organized, easy-to-navigate Content:
One way to further complicate cryptography is poor and unorganized documentation. Crypto Done Right aims to set a standard template around all content to help the audience become familiar with the structure of the website. After a few clicks around, you should find the website very easy to navigate through.

3. Community Driven:
The website will be using a centralized database where anyone can come in and contribute content. The content will automatically render according to the standard templates to maintain the overall feel of the website consistent.
