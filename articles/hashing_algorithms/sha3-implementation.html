Implementation Notes:

  • Move away from SHA-1 as soon as possible.
  • Consider Hardened SHA-1
  • HMAC-SHA1 is still considered safe.
  • dh-group14-sha1 is also still considered safe.
  • Identify assets that are most critical and focus on moving those out to something more strong and reliable like SHA-2.
  • Start by phasing out infrastructure where SHA-1 hashing is done for integrity check or digital signature.
